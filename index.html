Note: Anthropic API does not support browser (CORS) requests. Your roasts will fail unless you route through a CORS proxy. We recommend OpenAI or Google for hassle-free usage.

OpenAI:
  1. Go to platform.openai.com/api-keys

  3. Copy and paste here

Google:
  1. Go to aistudio.google.com/apikey
  2. Click "Create API key"
  3. Copy and paste here

Cost: free tier available

Anthropic:
  1. Go to console.anthropic.com/keys
  2. Click "Create Key"
  3. Copy and paste here

Cost: ~$0.01 per roast (requires CORS proxy)
